Foodly App

UX Research Wireframes Prototyping Design Systems Mobile UI

Foodly is a mobile app I created with a team in my User Experience Design class. We started with research and interviews, then moved into low fidelity sketches, wireframes, and the final interactive prototype in Figma. The goal was to make finding recipes and ingredients easier and more fun by combining useful features with a social platform feel. Users can post their own recipes, save ideas, share tips, and discover new meals through smart filtering.
